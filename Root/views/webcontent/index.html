Medical Tourism in India

Healthcare in developed countries remains far expensive in case of patients who have chronic ailments and make multiple visits to hospitals. Expensive and complex treatments like heart surgery, knee replacement and other orthopaedics treatments draw many medical travellers to India as the hospitals in India provides best and affordable care.Medical service in India offers treatments on par with countries like Germany, Israel at a lower cost. The cost of treatments is 2-3 times lower than hospitals in Europe. Compared to other countries medical treatments related to dental, orthopaedic, fertility, cardiology is very affordable in Indian hospitals and uses latest advancements in these areas.

Ayurveda, Panchakarma and other wellness treatments in India are very popular among patients from Europe, US and Middle East. The government of India have initiated various visa programs which help medical patients to get the medical visa to India in less time. Around 163 countries are eligible for e-visa service for India, saving time and money for the international patients visiting India.
